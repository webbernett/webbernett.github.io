About This Game

Me and my partner were tasked with recreating the NES classic "The Legend of Zelda" in Unity. I worked both in the Unity editor and inside many C# scripts for game objects. I created some of the enemies, (gels, wallmasters, spike traps) as well as many of Link's weapons (bow, boomerang, sword, sword beam). I also added sounds and sprites for better game authenticity, and did a lot of numbers tweaking to Link's scripts and enemy movement to get that classic NES feel.
